GEO vs SEO — what's the difference?

Both are about visibility. But they live in completely different places — and require different strategies to win.

GEO — Generative Engine Optimization

Where you appear
Inside AI-generated conversational answers — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity
User behaviour
User asks a question in natural language and reads a synthesised answer
Success metric
Citation count, citation rate, share of voice across AI platforms
What signals matter
Brand authority, content comprehensiveness, topical depth, source trustworthiness
Time to impact
2–8 weeks after publishing authoritative content
Trend
Rapidly growing — AI chat usage is up 400% YoY
🚀 The new channel to own
VS

SEO — Search Engine Optimization

Where you appear
On ranked lists of blue links in Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo search results pages
User behaviour
User scans a list of results and clicks to visit individual websites
Success metric
Keyword rankings, organic traffic, click-through rate, domain authority
What signals matter
Backlinks, page speed, on-page optimisation, E-E-A-T signals
Time to impact
3–12 months for competitive keywords
Trend
Mature, competitive — organic click share declining due to AI Overviews
